Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why is the product called "Kreme-a-Pepe"?

The name “Kreme-a-Pepe” playfully combines the words “Kreme” with the nickname of Jose Rizal, "Pepe,” which symbolizes the committment of the company in celebrating and promoting Filipino heritage through its unique offerings.
